Curry chicken recipe...

Ingredients...

  • chicken pieces
  • onion
  • garlic
  • curry powder
  • cinnamon
  • paprika
  • bay leaf
  • ginger
  • salt
  • yogurt
  • tomato paste
  • coconut milk

    Steps...

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Saute onion until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar and salt.
  4. Continue stirring for 2 minutes. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk.
  5. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
